see live site

Technologies: HTML, CSS, JavaScript, jQuery, Netlify

Libraries: Bootstrap

This website is a professional project for Makin Movez, a local moving company doing jobs throughout New England. The site was deployed on the Netlify CDN to ensure high speed loading for users at runtime. Netlify forms is used to process information requests from the website.
The primary focus of this project was to get the site deployed as soon as possible without compromising quality. The essential features of the site are to give potential customers information about the business as well as allow for them to contact the company for a quote. Among other reasons, the site was deployed on Netlify to utilize CI/CD as the site will continue to develop and grow as the business does the same.
